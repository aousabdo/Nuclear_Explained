Privacy Policy

Nuclear Explained (nukes.analyticadss.com) is committed to protecting your privacy. This page explains what data is collected and how it is used.

1. Analytics

This site uses Plausible Analytics — a privacy-respecting, cookie-free analytics service. Plausible does not:

  • Use cookies or persistent identifiers
  • Collect personally identifiable information
  • Track you across websites
  • Sell your data

Plausible collects only aggregate, anonymous metrics: page views, referrer, country (from IP, not stored), browser type, and device type. Your IP address is never logged or stored. See Plausible's privacy policy for full details.

2. No Cookies

This site does not set any cookies. Your preferences (mode and language) are stored only in your browser's URL hash (#casual-en) — they never leave your device.

3. Local Storage

No data is written to localStorage or sessionStorage.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's CDN. Google may log the request (IP, referrer). See Google's privacy policy.
  • OpenStreetMap / Leaflet — map tiles are loaded from OpenStreetMap tile servers. See OSM's privacy policy.
  • GitHub Pages — this site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may log standard web server access logs. See GitHub's privacy policy.

5. Educational Purpose

All content on this site is for educational purposes only. All data is sourced from publicly available, unclassified sources including Glasstone & Dolan (1977), the Federation of American Scientists, and the Bulletin of Atomic Scientists.
